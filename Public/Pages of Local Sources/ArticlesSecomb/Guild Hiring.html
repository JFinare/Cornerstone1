Join the Thorn's Vanguard Today!

An Adventuring Guild where the only requirement is your passion for adventure and experience!

We're an inclusive and diverse community of explorers, scholars, fighers and spellcasters who believe that your heritage and upbringing should never hold you back from embarking on epic quests. Our doors are open to all, whether you're a seasoned adventurer or just starting on your journey. With us, you'll explore dungeons, conquer fearsome monsters, discover arcane secrets and uncover long-lost treasures in the company of like-minded individuals.

It's not just about the adventures; it's also about the bonds you'll forge with your fellow members. We provide training and mentorship to help you rise to greatness and become a renowned hero of the land. No matter where you come from, the Adventurer's Guild is here to support you on your path to adventure and glory.

Join us today and be part of a community where judgment is left behind, and the thrill of the unknown awaits!
Our HQ is in Waterdeep, but We have recruitment stations in every major settlement.
