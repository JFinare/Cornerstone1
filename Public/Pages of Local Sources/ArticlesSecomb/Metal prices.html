Metal Prices Surge, Impacting Nearby Dwarven Clans

A Boom in the Market Puts Dwarven Craftsmen at a Crossroads

Metal prices are on the rise, and nearby dwarven clans famed for their metal craftsmanship are grappling with the consequences.

The unexpected spike in metal prices, fueled by high demand and depleted mines, is affecting the renowned dwarven forges. Clan leaders, like Bruenor Bronzebeard, acknowledge the opportunities for increased earnings but worry about the escalating cost of raw materials.

Authorities are exploring ways to support the clans during this economic shift, but without fresh mines to produce more raw metal, the dwarven artisans face a complex challenge: balancing their time-honored craft with the demands of a changing market. The future of their cherished traditions hangs in the balance as they navigate these uncharted waters.
